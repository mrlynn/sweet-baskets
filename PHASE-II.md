Sweet Baskets – Phase 2 Enhancement Plan

A practical roadmap to elevate the nonprofit’s digital presence

⸻

1. Hero Section Redesign (Clarity + Professional Polish)

Goal: Make the first 3 seconds unforgettable: clear mission, emotional resonance, and visual professionalism.

Deliverables
	•	Update hero layout to include:
	•	High-quality horizontal imagery (the ones we’ve generated)
	•	Soft lavender gradient overlay for readability
	•	Strong headline + subheadline
	•	Dual CTAs: Donate a Basket (primary), Get Involved (secondary)
	•	Add soft micro-interactions:
	•	Fade-in of headline on load
	•	Button lift/hover animation
	•	Add Sweet Baskets motif sparkles behind the headline (low-opacity, subtle).

⸻

2. Professional Footer Build-Out

Goal: Create a footer that signals legitimacy and trust.

Deliverables
	•	New footer with:
	•	Logo + 1–2 line mission statement
	•	Navigation links
	•	EIN and nonprofit status
	•	Partner/hospital logos (placeholder grayscale)
	•	Newsletter signup
	•	Social media links
	•	Add a pink border stripe at the top of the footer to match the logo outline.
	•	Add faint star/heart motif pattern in the background (5–8% opacity).

⸻

3. Impact Section (Homepage)

Goal: Show you’re a real, effective, credible nonprofit doing meaningful work.

Deliverables
	•	Impact stats:
	•	“Baskets Delivered: 3,214+”
	•	“Partner Facilities: 12”
	•	“Active Volunteers: 84”
	•	(Use placeholder numbers for now)
	•	Add animated counters.
	•	Add mini icons: hearts, sparkles, baskets (matching your logo aesthetic).
	•	Add 1–2 short story snippets (real or placeholders) from seniors or volunteers.

⸻

4. How It Works Section

Goal: Give donors and volunteers confidence through transparency.

Deliverables
	•	Three or four steps:
	1.	You Give
	2.	We Build
	3.	We Deliver
	4.	You Create Joy
	•	Add custom line-illustration icons (arch, hearts, sparkles).
	•	Add intersection fade-in reveal for each card.

⸻

5. Signature Brand Motif System

Goal: Turn the Sweet Baskets logo into a unified visual language across the site.

Motifs to implement
	•	The Arch – used as section dividers or top shape for testimonials.
	•	Pink border line – used in footer, CTA cards, section headings.
	•	Sparkles/Stars – small accents in hero + headers.
	•	Hearts – checkmarks, bullet points, counter icons.
	•	Basket weave pattern – extremely subtle, used as background texture in volunteers section.

⸻

6. Volunteer Page Upgrade

Goal: Make volunteer onboarding feel purposeful, simple, and inspiring.

Deliverables
	•	Short video or illustration explaining volunteering.
	•	One-page signup with:
	•	Availability
	•	Skills/interests
	•	Background check info (if needed in future)
	•	Success stories or testimonials.
	•	Add “Why We Need You” section for emotional clarity.

⸻

7. Donations Flow Optimization

Goal: Maximize conversions and trust.

Deliverables
	•	Suggested giving amounts:
	•	$25 = 1 basket
	•	$100 = 4 baskets
	•	$250 = 10 baskets
	•	Add toggle for:
	•	One-time
	•	Monthly giving
	•	Add brand assets to donor form so it doesn’t feel like a generic payment page.
	•	Add “Where Your Donation Goes” mini chart (placeholder).

⸻

8. Accessibility & Performance Pass

Goal: Ensure seniors, families, and medical partners can all use the site easily.

Deliverables
	•	WCAG AA pass:
	•	Color contrast checks
	•	Alt text for hero and all icons
	•	Keyboard-friendly navigation
	•	Image compression for speed
	•	Implement next/image everywhere
	•	Test for mobile readability and button tappability

⸻

9. Storytelling / Blog / Newssection

Goal: Build an ongoing narrative that grows trust and visibility.

Content types
	•	Delivery stories (“Margaret’s smile when her basket arrived…”)
	•	Volunteer spotlight
	•	Partner feature (hospital, nursing home, etc.)
	•	Founder story – Zoe Sweet’s vision
	•	Monthly or weekly updates

Deliverables
	•	Blog landing page
	•	Individual article template
	•	CMS or markdown-based posts for easy updates

⸻

10. SEO + Social Boost Pack

Goal: Increase discoverability and credibility across the web.

Deliverables
	•	Meta titles/descriptions for core pages
	•	OpenGraph images for social sharing
	•	Schema for nonprofits
	•	“Donate” structured data
	•	Google Search Console setup
	•	Instagram/TikTok/Facebook profile links in footer
	•	Add microcopy prompts:
	•	“Share this with someone who’d love to help.”

⸻

BONUS: Optional Add-Ons for Phase 2.5

If you want to go further after Phase 2:
	•	Add a Hospital Partner Portal (request baskets, schedule pickup/delivery).
	•	Build a Volunteer Hours Tracking System.
	•	Add Impact Map showing which communities you’ve served.

	•	Add Annual Report section to improve donor trust.
